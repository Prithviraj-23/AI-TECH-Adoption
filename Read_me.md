# AI & Tech Adoption Analytics - EDA Project

## Project Overview
This project explores the adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools across various industries. 
The goal is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to understand AI adoption trends, 
industry-wise adoption rates, and popular AI tools.

## Dataset
- Source: [Kaggle: Global AI Tool Adoption Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tfisthis/global-ai-tool-adoption-across-industries)
- Description: The dataset contains industry-wise AI adoption rates, popular AI tools, company size, and other relevant features.

# AI & Tech Adoption Analytics - Summary Report

## Key Insights

1. **Popular AI Tools**
   - The majority of companies are using tools like ChatGPT, MidJourney, and Bard.
   - Pie chart shows the percentage distribution of AI tool usage.

2. **AI Adoption by Industry**
   - Industries such as Technology and Finance have the highest AI adoption rates.
   - Some industries show lower adoption, which could be potential opportunities for AI expansion.
   - Box plots help identify outliers and spread of adoption rates.

3. **Company Size vs AI Adoption**
   - Scatter plots reveal that larger companies generally adopt AI more extensively.
   - Some small companies are also adopting AI at high rates, indicating early adoption trends.

4. **Average Adoption Rate**
   - Horizontal bar plots show industry-wise average adoption rates.
   - Helps quickly identify high-performing and low-performing sectors.

## Recommendations
- Focus on industries with low adoption rates to promote AI adoption.
- Promote popular AI tools with proven effectiveness across industries.
- Conduct deeper analysis on small companies adopting AI early for strategic insights.

## Conclusion
This EDA provides a clear understanding of AI adoption trends across industries, highlights popular tools, and identifies opportunities for further AI implementation.
